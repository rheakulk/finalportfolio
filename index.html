About Me

Hey again! Like I said, my name's Rhea

I’m a UX designer who loves working with people and technology. I have a technical foundation in programming that allows me to both empathize with the needs of the end-user as well as understand the technical requirements of a project.

Thus, I ensure both are considered when delivering a final product. As a designer, I firmly believe in adopting a user-centered design approach that anchor the end-user at the heart of every decision I make.

I thrive on projects that allow me to leverage my technical and visual design skills, as well as my genuine excitement for working with users to understand their needs on a human level.

I ultimately strive to find ways to seamlessly integrate technology into people’s lives and deliver experiences that are intuitive, meaningful, and innovative.

When I’m not working, you’ll find me taking long, aimless walks to explore whatever city I’m in and finding coffee shops, scoping out my next favorite ramen spot, or re-reading Game of Thrones for the hundredth time.
